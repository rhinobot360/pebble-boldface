BoldFace

Privacy Policy

Overview

BoldFace is a watchface for Pebble smartwatches. We take your privacy seriously and aim to be transparent about what limited data we collect and why.

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties beyond the services described below.

What We Collect

BoldFace periodically sends an anonymous ping to help us understand how the watchface is being used in the real world. This may include:

  • Active color theme
  • Enabled features (weather, date, steps)
  • Visibility preference (Always On, On Shake, Off)
  • App version

These pings contain no personally identifiable information and are logged to a private Google Sheet accessible only to the developer.

Location Data

If you enable the weather feature, BoldFace requests your device's GPS coordinates to fetch local weather from Open-Meteo, a free and open-source weather service. Your coordinates are sent directly to Open-Meteo and are not stored by BoldFace. Open-Meteo's privacy policy is available at open-meteo.com/en/terms.

Data Retention

Usage ping data logged to Google Sheets is retained indefinitely but contains no personally identifiable information.

Children's Privacy

BoldFace does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date below.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through the Pebble app store listing for BoldFace.
